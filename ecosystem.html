Importance In An Ecosystem

This Is A Large Amount Of Reasons Bees Are Important

  1. The honeybees wax brought the first artificial light from candles.
  2. The honeybees provided the first seals and provided the first modeling material
  3. Einstein said "If the bee disappeared from the surface of the globe then man would only have 4 years of life left."
  4. For the reasons above beekeeping was developed
  5. Beekeeping is very important to the economy
  6. Farmers in China work extra hours to hand pollinate what would take minutes if done by honeybees.
  7. Colony Collapse Disorder has killed a third of all US honeybees.
  8. The bees could vanish by 2035.
  9. The honeybee population could be wiped out in ten years in the UK.
  10. Germany's dropped from 4 million hives to 800,000.
  11. The 10 crops that depend most on honeybees are apples, almonds, cotton, citrus, soya beans, onions, broccoli, carrots, sunflowers, and melons. (In order from most to greatest)
  12. Without bees we can't get any fruits besides bananas and pineapples.
  13. Without bees there are no vegetables.
  14. Also no meat or dairy products
  15. The two above have a few exceptions
  16. Ther would be a great decrease in coffee.
  17. The only two cooking oils left would be walnut and olive.
  18. Most clothes would be gone.
  19. Most medicine would be gone.
  20. Bees give farmers lots of crops.
  21. While gathering nectar, bees get pollen on their legs and when they go to their next flower they drop the pollen off, pollinating that flower.
  22. Honey is used in some medicines, along with pollen.
